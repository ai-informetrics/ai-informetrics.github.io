Special Issue in Scientometrics - AI + Informetrics (AII2021)


Accepted submissions at 1st Workshop on AI + Informetrics (AII2021) at the iConference2021 will be invited to submit extended papers to our special issue in Scientometrics .
The open call for papers at Scientometrics.

Submission Details and Deadlines

  • July 15, 2021: All submissions due
    • Original content typeset abiding by the Scientometrics Authors' Guidelines
    • Submission via the  Scientometrics Editorial Manager under “S.I. : AI + Informetrics” 
  • 1st round of reviews
    • September 1, 2021: Referees' reports with decision (accept, minor/major modifications, reject) sent to authors 
    • November 5, 2021: Revised manuscripts due
  • 2nd round of reviews
    • January 10, 2022: Referees' 2nd reports with decision (accept, minor/major modifications, reject) sent to authors
    • March 15, 2022: Revised manuscripts due
  • Online publication with citable DOI in Early View: 3-4 weeks upon acceptance
  • Publication in the Special Issue of Scientometrics: Summer 2022
